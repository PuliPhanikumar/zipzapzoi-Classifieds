Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before posting your first ad.

Can I really post 6 ads for free? expand_more

Yes! Every new user gets 6 free ad credits in their first 30 days — no credit card, no catch. After that, you continue to get 1 free ad every month forever.

What happens after my 6 free ads are used? expand_more

You'll still get 1 free ad every month automatically. For more ads, choose Extra Ad (₹19), Renewal (₹16), or a Business Pack starting at ₹149 for 10 ads.

Can I create multiple accounts to get more free ads? expand_more

No. Our anti-spam system tracks one free trial per mobile number and device. Duplicate accounts are detected and suspended automatically.

Is ZipZapZoi available in my city? expand_more

ZipZapZoi works all across India. Just set your location when posting and buyers near you will find your listing. We especially focus on Tier-2 & Tier-3 cities.

How long does it take for my ad to go live? expand_more

Our moderation team reviews every listing within 2 hours. You'll get a notification when your ad is live and visible to buyers.

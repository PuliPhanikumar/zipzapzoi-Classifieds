Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really post 6 ads for free? expand_more

Yes! Every new user gets 6 free ads in their first 30 days. After that, you get 1 free ad per month plus affordable paid plans starting at just ₹16.

What happens after my free trial ends? expand_more

You continue to get 1 free ad per month. If you need more, our Extra Ad plan at ₹19/ad is one of the most affordable in India. Expired ads can be renewed for just ₹16.

Can I create multiple accounts to get more free ads? expand_more

No. Our anti-spam system allows only one free trial per mobile number and device. Duplicate accounts will be automatically flagged and suspended.

Do paid plans expire if I don't use all ads? expand_more

Unused ads do not expire as long as your plan validity is active. After validity ends, you'll need to renew or purchase a new plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a free ad? expand_more
Every user gets 3 free ads per month! Simply log in to your account, go to the Seller Dashboard, and click the "Post Ad" button. Follow the easy steps to add photos, set a price, and publish.
Are payments on ZipZapZoi secure? expand_more
Yes, completely! We use Razorpay, an industry-leading payment gateway. We support UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Wallets. We never store your card details on our servers.
How does the refund policy work? expand_more
If your plan fails to activate after a successful payment, or if there is a duplicate charge, we will automatically refund the amount within 5-7 business days. Please refer to our Refund Policy for more details.
How do I edit or delete my listing? expand_more
Go to your Seller Dashboard, find the active listing, and click the "Edit" or "Delete" icon. Changes will reflect instantly on the platform.
How can I protect myself from scams? expand_more
Always meet in public, safe locations. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or sensitive bank details with a buyer or seller. If a deal seems too good to be true, it probably is. Always verify the item in person before making a payment.
